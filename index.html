About Us

A new AWS-based open-source project, called CatchNoise, is provided as a foundational project allowing governments and their technology partners to build portable, rugged and tamperproof devices that can be trained to learn different noise types, log evidence in a manner that maintains a legal chain-of-evidence and alert on noise in real-time. This will give governments and citizen’s confidence in making claims of noise pollution.

With cities becoming more densely populated and the mix of dwelling and construction constantly changing it is challenging for governments to address noise complaints raised by their citizens. For example, residents in detached housing often complain about new apartment blocks and the changes this brings in traffic and even air-conditioning noise. Another example is where residents move into an area with a strong pre-existing local musical culture that causes them ongoing disturbance.

Catchnoise is a cloud application taht is designed to be used by citizens and authority to help manage noise pollution and to help achieve a peaceful life for eveyone.
